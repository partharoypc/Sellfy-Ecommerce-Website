Still have some questions?

Here are some of the answers you might be looking for.

Does Sellfy charge a transaction fee?

No, Sellfy does not charge a transaction fee. When you choose a plan that works for you depending on your yearly transaction volume, no additional fees will be charged.

Will I have any other expenses?

On top of your monthly payment for Sellfy, you will need to cover payment processing fees of PayPal or Stripe (typically 2.9% + 30¢) depending on which payment gateway you choose.

What happens if I go over yearly sales cap?

If you do exceed the limit for your current subscription plan, you will be expected to upgrade to the next subscription plan. We've specifically designed the tools and features for each plan to best assist you and your customers and we’ll make sure to notify you if you have reached the amount of maximum revenue set for your plan. But, if the account has not upgraded, we may start to charge a 2% overage fee for the amount of revenue that has gone over the subscription plan limits.

What's email credits?

Email credits are the number of emails that can be sent using our built-in email marketing feature. You will get a set number of credits to use each month with either the Business or Premium plan. If you exceed this limit, you can purchase more credits from merchant dashboard.

Do I need to have a website to use Sellfy?

No, you can build a store on Sellfy and start selling instantly on Facebook, Twitter, YouTube or any other social network. If you have a website, you can embed the "buy now" buttons, product widgets or the full Sellfy store anywhere on your site.

How do I get paid?

You will receive money instantly after a purchase is made. Payments will be sent directly to your PayPal or Stripe account depending on which gateway you choose for payment processing.

What are the limits for digital products?

Total number of digital products is unlimited. One digital product can contain up to 50 files, with an overall maximum size of 10 GB each.

What does migration mean?

There are three types of migration, two of which are only available on select plans and each is intended to be a one-time service.

Email migration is available with all plans and with this, we'll import your email contacts to the built-in Marketing feature. (completed in 1-2 business days)

Product migration is available with the Business and Premium subscriptions. We'll assist in uploading your product files and/or product descriptions for you. (completed in 5-20 business days)

Store design migration is available with an annual Business or Premium subscription. This means that our team can help you design your storefront by taking your current website design elements and incorporating them into your new Sellfy store. (completed in 5-20 business days)
